ABOUT US

Welcome to our learning platform! We are a passionate team dedicated to helping people grow through technology. Our mission is to make programming easy, fun, and accessible to everyone — regardless of experience, and exclusively for people over 18 years old. Led by Allison Pauleth, a creative and inspiringdeveloper, we work to guide you step by step on your learning journey.Join us and be part of a community that turns curiosity into code!

REQUIREMENTS & ENROLLMENT

To enroll in our programming courses, you must be 18 years or older and have basic computer skills.No previous experience in coding is required — just curiosity and the motivation to learn! The enrollment process is simple: fill out the registration form, choose your course, and make your payment through our secure platform. Once completed, you will receive a confirmation email with access to your course materials. Join us and take the first step toward your future in tech!
